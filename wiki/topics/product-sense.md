# Product Sense

## Framework for Product Questions
1. **Clarify**: what product, who are users, what's the goal?
2. **Define metrics**: primary (OEC), secondary, guardrails
3. **Analyze**: segment by user type, platform, geography
4. **Recommend**: data-driven recommendation with trade-offs

## Common Metrics
- **Engagement**: DAU, MAU, DAU/MAU ratio, session length, retention
- **Revenue**: ARPU, LTV, conversion rate, churn rate
- **Quality**: NPS, CSAT, task success rate, time-to-complete
- **Growth**: new users, viral coefficient, referral rate

## Metric Selection
- Good metric: measurable, actionable, relevant to business goal
- Avoid vanity metrics (total registered users, page views)
- Consider counter-metrics (guardrails) to prevent gaming

## Trade-off Patterns
- Short-term revenue vs long-term retention
- Engagement vs quality (clickbait problem)
- Personalization vs exploration (filter bubble)
- Speed vs accuracy
